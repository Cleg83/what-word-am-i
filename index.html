Welcome to WWAMI

(What Word Am I?)

How to play:

  • Guess the word by inputting letters into the blank tiles,
    when you are happy with your guess click "Submit"
  • Guess the word without a hint: 5 points
  • Guess the word with 1 hint: 3 points
  • Guess the word with 2 hints: 2 points
  • Guess the word with 3 hints: 1 points
  • Pass: 0 points
